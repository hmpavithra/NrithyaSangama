Nrithya Sangama dance academy founded by Preetha Arun provides personalized instruction for the serious student who aspires to excel in classical Bharatanatyam. Dance lessons at Nrithya Sangama follow a comprehensive, well-rounded, immersive curriculum that places emphasis on quality and encourages the pursuit of excellence.
The Bharatanatyam curriculum includes instruction in Indian Classical Dance theory and cultural history, fitness and body conditioning, and rigorous fundamental training and technique refinement.
